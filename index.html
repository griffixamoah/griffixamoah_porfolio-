About Me
Passion Meets
Precision

I'm Griffix Amoah, a graphic designer trained at the Takoradi Technical University, Faculty of Applied Arts and Technology, Ghana. I craft visual communication that is bold, purposeful and memorable.

My work spans corporate branding, print media, product packaging, publications, and event design. Whether it's a small business flyer or a full institutional brochure, I bring the same level of care and creativity to every project.

Based in Agona Swedru, Central Region, I serve clients across Ghana and beyond.
